Tel-A-Friend

Tel-A-Friend, Country-Style Motel

Located within walking distance of downtown Vernon shops and parks, the Tel-A-Friend Country Motel brings you 50' eclectic charm and reasonable rates. Whether you are in Vernon for business or pleasure, the Tel-A-Friend Motel is a convenient and comfortable spot to stay. Skiing, biking, hiking, golfing, and wine tours, Tel-A-Friend is close to it all!

Our rooms are clean, homey and comfortable and every room has it's own kitchenette. We also offer free Wifi for your browsing needs. Check out the local attractions page for information on food services and things to do.

Local Attractions

Nearby Attractions

In the winter, Vernon is home to one of the world's best family-friendly ski resorts, Silver Star Mountain. Just a short 29 minute drive from Tel-A-Friend, Silver Star offers world class downhill skiiling, cross country skiing, snowboarding and snowshoeing and many events. During the summer, it is also a world class mountain biking destination with an excellent mountain biking park.

Vernon is home to some fantastic golf resorts from small to world class. The nearest golf course is Vernon Golf & Country Club, a short 5 minute drive away. Hillview Golf course is only 6 minutes away. World-class Predator Ridge Resort is only 13 minutes away!

Wine tours are another popular activity in the area. There are some great local winerys near the Tel-A-Friend, check out MJO Tours for some great wine discovery experiences.

Hiking and Biking

Vernon is very hiker and biker friendly. There are plenty of great routes to ride and some amazing places to hike. Check out Vernon's top 10 trails.
