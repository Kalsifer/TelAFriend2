Tel-A-Friend, Country-Style Motel

Located within walking distance of downtown Vernon shops and parks, the Tel-A-Friend Country Motel brings you 50' eclectic charm and reasonable rates. Whether you are in Vernon for business or pleasure, the Tel-A-Friend Motel is a convenient and comfortable spot to stay. Skiing, biking, hiking, golfing, and wine tours, Tel-A-Friend is close to it all!

Our rooms are clean, homey and comfortable and every room has it's own kitchenette. We also offer free Wifi for your browsing needs. Check out the local attractions page for information on food services and things to do.
