Tel-A-Friend, Country-Style Motel

Located within walking distance of downtown Vernon shops and parks, and only blocks from Vernon Jubilee Hospital, the Tel-A-Friend Country Motel brings you 50' eclectic charm and reasonable rates. Whether you are in Vernon for business or pleasure, the Tel-A-Friend Motel is a convenient and comfortable spot to stay. Skiing, biking, hiking, golfing, and wine tours, Tel-A-Friend is close to it all!

Our suites are clean, homey and comfortable and every suite has it's own kitchenette. We also offer free Wifi for your browsing needs. Check out the local attractions page for information on food services and things to do.

Room Information

About Our suites

Every room is a one-bedroom suite at the Tel-a-Friend hotel. A coffee maker, a work desk, and a sofa are provided in every suite. An en suite bathroom includes free toiletries. You can access the garden seating or just enjoy garden views from your suite. A gas furnace offers heating in all suites. A guest launderette is offered at Motel Tel-A-Friend. A vending machine provides drinks. Fax and photocopying facilities are available on site. Free on-site parking and WIFI is included.

Places to Eat

Food Services

Want to make use of our kitchenettes? The Tel-a-Friend Motel is 4 blocks from Village Market and Deli. Not in the mood to cook? We are located nearly right across from Jim's Place Pizza. We also have vending machines on the premises for a quick snack.



Local Attractions

Nearby Attractions

Skiing, Snowboarding, Golf

In the winter, Vernon is home to one of the world's best family-friendly ski resorts, Silver Star Mountain. Just a short 29 minute drive from Tel-A-Friend, Silver Star offers world class downhill skiiling, cross country skiing, snowboarding and snowshoeing and many events. During the summer, it is also a world class mountain biking destination with an excellent mountain biking park.

Vernon is home to some fantastic golf resorts from small to world class. The nearest golf course is Vernon Golf & Country Club, a short 5 minute drive away. Hillview Golf course is only 6 minutes away. World-class Predator Ridge Resort is only 13 minutes away!

Wine tours are another popular activity in the area. There are some great local winerys near the Tel-A-Friend, check out MJO Tours for some great wine discovery experiences.

Hiking and Biking

Vernon is very hiker and biker friendly. There are plenty of great routes to ride and some amazing places to hike. Check out Vernon's top 10 trails.

We are across from an excellent multi-use path that goes from Okanagan College to Okanagan Landing. There is also a dog park and a walking path right across the street. We are just blocks from Polson Park, Vernon's oldest and largest public park. Polson park offers a skate park, tennis courts, a playground, ducks and swans, lawn bowling, and outdoor exercise equipment. You can also check out the Okanagan Science Centre while you are there.
